About Us

Built for owners who need one number to call.

ATS Total Service Pty. Ltd. is a Sydney-based property maintenance company built to consolidate the everyday operations of owning and managing property. Cleaning, gardening, waste collection and painting — delivered by one team, on one invoice, with one accountable contact.

With our own fleet of trucks, vans and commercial-grade machinery, we operate without subcontractors. That means consistent quality, predictable scheduling, and direct accountability — the operating standard that property managers, strata committees and commercial landlords expect.

Process

From enquiry to handover
in three steps.

A simple, transparent process designed for property managers and busy owners who need things done right the first time.

01

Enquiry & Site Review

Tell us about your property and what needs attention. We respond within one business day with clarifying questions and arrange a site visit if needed.

02

Quote & Schedule

You receive a clear, itemized quote with no hidden fees and a confirmed schedule. For ongoing contracts, we propose a maintenance plan tailored to your property.

03

Delivery & Handover

Our own crew arrives in our own vehicles with our own equipment. Work is completed to spec, the site is left tidy, and you receive a handover with before/after photos on request.
